Meet the Legend

Andy Famiglietti is the man, the myth, and the legend. Some people refer to him as Afam. Others say he is the new messiah. This sweet sweet man is a teacher, developer and media scholar. He holds an American Culture Studies PhD from Bowling Green State University. He teaches Communications Ethics and Web Design, and graces people with his presence at St. Joseph’s University.
Wikipedia, Free Software, Copyright Law, and digitally-mediated culture study and write about him. He hacks on Marca and organizes for the Calliope initiative in his spare time. He’s getting pretty weirded out at the fact this is a website.
Also, Andy is an active member of community service. Not only does he spend his time cleaning up the web, he also cleans up the streets. Students and people with messy cars need his help to make their lives better. Andy does it all.
Finally, when Andy isn't spending his time fighting crime or helping students, he visits old people and helps them write films as you can see from this wonderful chinshot.
